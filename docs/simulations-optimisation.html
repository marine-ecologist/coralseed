Optimising larval releases: how many larvae are needed to achieve a pre-determined density of coral settlers?

2024-01-29

coralseed outputs from a dispersal model of 1000 particles released from a simulated larval release at sheltered bay site at Lizard Island (nGBR) in December 2022. Larvae are released from the centre of the restoraton hectare (red box) at mid-tide (17/12/22) and are tracked across a 720 minute timespan following release.
